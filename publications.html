Q1 Journal 2025

Mesozoic Muslim Bagh-Khanozai ophiolite melange in the western Pakistan: implications for the tectonic evolution of the Neo-Tethys Ocean

Khan, A.U., Dong, Y.P.*, Rehman, S.U., Hui, B., Usman, M., Panezai, M.

Journal of Asian Earth Sciences (Scopus Q1)

This study investigates the Mesozoic Muslim Bagh-Khanozai ophiolite melange, providing crucial insights into the tectonic evolution of the Neo-Tethys Ocean. Through comprehensive geochemical and geochronological analysis, we constrain the formation mechanisms and emplacement history of these ophiolitic complexes.

DOI: 10.1016/j.jseaes.2025.106737
Q2 Journal 2025

Geochemistry and Geochronology of Naweoba Ophiolitic Mélange in Western Pakistan: Constrains on the Tectonics of Neo-Tethys Ocean

Khan, A.U., Dong, Y.P.*, Rehman, S.U., Hui, B., Usman, M., Panezai, M.

Geological Journal (Scopus Q2)

This research presents detailed geochemical and geochronological data from the Naweoba ophiolitic mélange, offering new constraints on the tectonic history of the Neo-Tethys Ocean. The study utilizes LA-ICP-MS U-Pb dating and whole-rock geochemistry to understand the petrogenesis and tectonic setting.

DOI: 10.1002/gj.70172
In Progress 2026

Mantle and Crustal Contributions to the Muslim Bagh-Khanozai and Zhob Ophiolites: Insights from Zircon U-Pb Geochronology and Sr-Nd-Hf Isotopes

Khan, A.U., Dong, Y.P.*

Manuscript in Preparation

This ongoing research investigates the mantle and crustal contributions to the Muslim Bagh-Khanozai and Zhob ophiolites using advanced isotopic techniques. The study combines zircon U-Pb geochronology with Sr-Nd-Hf isotope analysis to constrain magma sources and crustal evolution.
