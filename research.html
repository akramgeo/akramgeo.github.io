Research Projects

2019 - 2025 PhD Research

Tectonic Evolution of Pakistani Himalaya and other Orogenic Belts

Comprehensive investigation of the tectonic evolution of the Pakistani Himalaya and associated orogenic belts through multi-disciplinary approaches. The research integrates extensive field studies, high-precision geochronology (LA-ICP-MS U-Pb dating), whole-rock and isotopic geochemistry (Sr-Nd-Hf), and structural analysis to constrain the formation, evolution, and emplacement of ophiolitic complexes in western Pakistan. Key focus areas include the Muslim Bagh-Khanozai, Zhob, and Naweoba ophiolites, providing crucial insights into Neo-Tethyan oceanic crust formation, subduction processes, and continental collision dynamics.
